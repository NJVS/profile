Aspiring Web Developer!

I've always wanted to work in the web development industry since I find its technology fascinating, and becoming a Full-Stack Developer is my ultimate objective. Tho I'm proud of my front-end skills, I'm well aware that I still have a lot to learn.

I earned my bachelor's degree in computer science from Lyceum of the Philippines University. I chose this major because programming is, for me, the perfect balance of logic and creativity. Starting from a blank canvas, I can give life to my ideas. I've always been interested in web development, but during my undergrad studies, I mostly handled the data structure and back-end on almost all the projects I worked on and rarely worked on web development.

Although I have no professional experience in web development, I have worked on projects with other developers in the past. I also wrote some apps to help analyze streamflow for a friend's project. I wasn't paid for it (probono) and was done in my spare time. I love the fact that I can get paid to do something that I would do for free. I love solving puzzles, and that is what programming really is.
